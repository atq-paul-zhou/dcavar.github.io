This is an initial draft of an online textbook for computational, corpus, quantitative and theoretical linguists, who want to make use of Python 3 for their educational, research and applied projects.

The material presented here was developed over the last years from course material and code generated for research purposes. It was initially created for Python 1.x, then adapted to Python 2.x, and finally here made available for Python 3.x.

Currently, all sections and the included code are under active development.
